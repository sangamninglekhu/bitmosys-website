The meaning behind.

What's in the name?

The word “Bitmosys” is derived from two different words viz. bit and osmosis which refers to the movement of bit/codes in that we provide highly secure and robust codes for our valued clients.

The world is shifting from web2.0 to web3.0 and our mission is to create the infrastructures for that movement by helping companies build their next-gen softwares. Our team comprises highly skilled engineers and scientists from around the world who can deliver the highest quality projects on budget and on time.

Our mission

Blockchain today can be compared to the early internet era with the majority of the people left unknown and skeptical about the technology. However, we firmly believe that the future will be the one with blockchain technology embraced in most of the day-to-day life, transforming lives, and how business is done.

Our mission, therefore, is firmly aligned to resonate with that belief that can be broken down into:

  • Community build-up
  • Mass adoption of blockchain technology

  • Incubation programs
